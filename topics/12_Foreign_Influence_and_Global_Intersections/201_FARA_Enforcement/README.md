# FARA Enforcement

Purpose: Explains how the Foreign Agents Registration Act's civil enforcement culture, LDA exemption pathways, and high criminal intent standard have produced minimal deterrence relative to the volume of foreign-principal-directed lobbying activity, and what high-profile prosecutions (Manafort, Flynn) revealed about systemic under-registration.
