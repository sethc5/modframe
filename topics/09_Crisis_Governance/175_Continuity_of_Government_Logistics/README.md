# Continuity of Government Logistics

Purpose: Maps how COG logistics maintain classified alternate facilities, communication systems, and personnel procedures in perpetual readiness, but classification prevents evaluation of whether the system would work during actual catastrophic events.

Status: draft

Tags: COG, continuity, logistics, facilities, communications, COOP, Raven Rock, Mount Weather, classified

Outline: see `outline.md`

## Key actors

- FEMA (civilian COOP program, Mount Weather)
- DOD (military COG facilities, Raven Rock, Cheyenne Mountain)
- White House Military Office (presidential COG logistics)
- DISA (communications infrastructure)
- Agency COOP coordinators
- Secret Service (succession chain protection)

## Statutes and authorities

- FEMA Federal Continuity Directives (FCD 1, FCD 2)
- NSPD-51/HSPD-20 (National Continuity Policy)
- DOD Defense Continuity Program directives

## Case studies

- 9/11 COG activation (PEOC, Raven Rock, Air Force One)
- Eagle Horizon continuity exercises
- Agency COOP compliance audits
