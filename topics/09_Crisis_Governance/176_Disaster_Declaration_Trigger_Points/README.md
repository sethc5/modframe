# Disaster Declaration Trigger Points

Purpose: Maps how disaster declaration trigger points use damage thresholds that appear objective but are non-binding guidelines within a discretionary presidential process, allowing political considerations to influence aid allocation.

Status: draft

Tags: disaster declaration, FEMA, PDA, trigger points, thresholds, presidential discretion, damage assessment

Outline: see `outline.md`

## Key actors

- FEMA PDA teams (damage assessment)
- FEMA Regional and HQ staff (recommendation chain)
- The President (final declaration authority)
- State emergency management (PDA participation)
- Local officials (damage reporting)

## Statutes and authorities

- Stafford Act (42 U.S.C. §§ 5121–5207)
- FEMA PDA process guidance and per-capita indicators
- Federal Register, damage indicator adjustments

## Case studies

- Declarations below and above per-capita thresholds
- PDA deployment delays during concurrent disasters
- Governor declaration request denials and appeals
