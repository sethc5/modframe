# Defense Production Act Deployment Process

Purpose: Maps the operational chain from presidential DPA delegation through agency implementation to industry compliance, showing how institutional capacity gaps attenuate the DPA's theoretical mobilization power.

Status: draft

Tags: DPA, deployment, rated orders, DPAS, supply chain, production, institutional capacity, COVID-19

Outline: see `outline.md`

## Key actors

- Agency DPA program offices (order issuance)
- DPAS administrators (BIS/DOD)
- Company compliance officers (order execution)
- Supply chain managers (priority cascading)
- FEMA Supply Chain Stabilization Task Force
- Interagency coordination bodies

## Statutes and authorities

- Defense Production Act of 1950 (50 U.S.C. §§ 4501–4568)
- DPAS regulations (15 CFR Part 700)
- 50 U.S.C. § 4513 (compliance penalties)
